## üìù Image Specifications

### Technical Requirements:
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Resolution**: Minimum 800x600 pixels, recommended 1200x800 pixels
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB per image for fast loading
- **Aspect Ratio**: Preferably 4:3 or 16:9 for consistent display

### Content Guidelines:
- **Clarity**: Images should be clear and easy to understand
- **Labels**: Consider adding text labels or arrows where helpful
- **Language**: German labels are authentic but English is fine for educational content
- **Rights**: Ensure you have rights to use images (use Creative Commons, public domain, or create your own)

## üé® Creating Your Own Images

### Tools for Creating Diagrams:
- **Canva** (free tier available) - Great for infographics
- **Draw.io / diagrams.net** (free) - Excellent for technical diagrams
- **PowerPoint/Google Slides** - Simple diagrams and export as images
- **GIMP** (free) - Photo editing
- **Figma** (free tier) - Professional design tool

### Finding Free Images:
- **Unsplash** - High-quality free photos
- **Pexels** - Free stock photos
- **Pixabay** - Free images and illustrations
- **Wikimedia Commons** - Educational images
- **Flaticon** - Icons and simple illustrations

### Creating Waste Separation Images:
For German waste bins, consider:
1. Take photos of actual bins in Germany (if available)
2. Create illustrations showing bin colors and examples
3. Use official waste management websites for reference
4. German waste management companies often have educational materials

## üöÄ Quick Start (Without Images)

The quiz works perfectly without images! Questions with `image: null` will simply not display an image section. You can:

1. **Start using the quiz immediately** - All questions work without images
2. **Add images gradually** - Add one image at a time as you create/find them
3. **Test with placeholders** - Use simple colored rectangles as placeholders
4. **Focus on content** - Questions are comprehensive and educational on their own
