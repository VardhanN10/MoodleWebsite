## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Start with Canva** - It's free, easy, and has trash can icons
2. **Use consistent style** - Same background color, similar layouts
3. **Add text labels** - Makes images self-explanatory
4. **Keep it simple** - Clean, clear images work better than complex ones
5. **Test on mobile** - Make sure text is readable on small screens
